About us.

Spectral is a small renovation studio in Boise. We work on kitchens, baths, full-floor remodels, and the custom interior work that finishes them off.

We keep the project list short on purpose. It lets the owner stay close to every job, every week, from your first email to the day we hand the house back.

What we do.

Every project gets the same level of work and attention, whether you are remodeling a powder room or rebuilding a whole floor.

Bathroom Renovations

Primary baths, guest baths, and powder rooms. Smart layouts, quiet plumbing, and tile that still looks right in five years.

Whole-Home Remodeling

Multi-room and full-floor remodels that hold together as one design. One palette, one project lead, and a schedule built around your family living through it.

Custom Interior Upgrades

Built-ins, paneling, libraries, mudrooms, and custom millwork. The interior work that turns a house you already live in into one that fits how you live.

High-End Finish Installations

Targeted finish upgrades. New stone, new lighting, new hardware. The kind of work that changes how a room looks without rebuilding it.

Design-Build Consultation

Early-stage planning. We talk through design direction, real scope, and a realistic budget before anyone breaks ground.

How we work.

Most renovations go sideways because nobody tells the homeowner what is happening. We fix that.

You will get a written update from us every Friday. It tells you what we did this week, what is coming next week, and what decisions need to happen on your end. Selections and approvals get scheduled in advance so they do not catch you off guard during construction. If something will affect the budget or the timeline, you hear about it in writing before it happens.

The person who scoped your project also runs your job site, and walks you through the finished work at the end. There is no project manager you have never met. Just the owner, on your house, every week.
