The founder

The founder.

I founded this company with one mission in mind: to bring a higher standard of craftsmanship, transparency, and professionalism to residential remodeling. With years of hands-on experience ranging from custom renovation work to managing large-scale residential and commercial construction projects as a carpenter, project engineer or superintendent, I understand what it takes to deliver exceptional results without cutting corners.

My background has taught me that successful remodeling is built on clear communication, attention to detail, and executing every phase of construction with precision. I approach every project with the same level of care, organization, and quality control that major builders rely on for multi-million-dollar developments. When you choose Spectral Renovation & Design, you're choosing a builder who combines professional construction expertise with a genuine commitment to treating your home as if it were my own.
