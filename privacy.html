Privacy Policy

How Spectral Renovation & Design collects, uses, and protects what you share through this website. A plain-language draft, to be reviewed by counsel before launch.

Draft for launch · To be finalized before publication

What we collect

When you submit the consultation form or contact us through this website, we collect what you provide. That usually includes:

  • Your name and contact details
  • Project location, scope, budget range, and timing
  • Any inspiration or project photos you choose to share
  • Basic analytics such as device, pages viewed, and how you found us

How we use it

We use your information only to review fit, respond to your inquiry, and talk through a possible project. That means reviewing service area and scope, scheduling next steps, improving the website, and meeting legal requirements where they apply.

What we do not do

We do not sell your information. We do not share project details with third parties for their marketing. We do not add you to mailing lists you did not ask for.

How we store it

Form submissions are sent over a secure connection and kept in the tools we use to run the business, such as email, scheduling, and a CRM. Access is limited to Spectral and those platforms.

Cookies and analytics

This website uses cookies and analytics to understand how visitors use the site. The data is aggregated and used to improve the experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Your choices

You can ask for a copy of what we hold, ask us to correct or delete it, or withdraw consent to be contacted, by emailing the address on our contact page.

Contact

Questions about this policy can go to the email on our contact page. The final version will be reviewed before launch and posted here.
